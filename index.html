Financial Calculations FAQ

What is the Equated Monthly Investment (EMI) compound logic?

EMI processes interest using standard reducing balances where values are continually adjusted based on principal balances outstanding monthly.

How frequently does a standard bank FD compound interest?

By default, regular commercial banks compute fixed deposit interest intervals using standard quarterly compounding structures.
